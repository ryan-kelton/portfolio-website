Beyond Ramen

Project Overview

Beyond Ramen is a recipe sharing website for young adults seeking better food alternatives. Without any existing creative direction, they were in need of a brand visual style. Once complete, I designed and developed their responsive website prototype.

To test the prototype, click to view the live site on GitHub. On the home page of the site click on "Tuna and caper pasta with crispy breadcrumbs" under "New Recipes".

Reflection

I learned a great deal about metadata while working on this project. I used Schema.org to write semantic html while developing the recipe page.
