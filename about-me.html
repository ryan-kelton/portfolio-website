About Me

I'm Ryan Kelton, a UX/UI designer.

My mission is to help people interact with digital products and to create memorable and pleasant experiences online. I am a graduate student at Arizona State University, studying Graphic Information Technology.

From as far back as I can remember, I have always loved learning how things work and creating new things of my own. But with age and experience, came a new perspective on design. As a UX/UI designer, I now seek to use and develop my skills and knowledge to help other people have the best experience online.

On a personal level I am an avid reader, continual learner and musician on Spotify.

If you haven't checked out my portfolio or resume yet, I encourage you to go and give those a look. You are welcome to send me an email if you're interested in working with me, my schedule is open.
