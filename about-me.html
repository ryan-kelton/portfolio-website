About Me

Hello there. I'm Ryan, a UX/UI designer.

My mission is to help people interact with digital products and to create memorable and pleasant experiences online. I am a graduate student at Arizona State University, studying Graphic Information Technology.

My design journey began at a young age. It started with Legos, grew into a love of architecture and reached its height in brand identity. Or so I thought. At university I discovered usability design, and it became my new obsession. With experience in customer service and a background in graphic design, I had found a way to marry the two.

On a personal level, I am an avid reader, continual learner and musician(Spotify Link).
